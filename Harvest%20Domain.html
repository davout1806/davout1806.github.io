1st Level: Labor of the Land

Starting when you select this domain at 1st level, you gain proficiency with mauls, nets, scythes, tridents, war picks, and warhammers. As well as with your choice of two of the following: brewer's supplies, carpenter's tools, cook's utensils, weaver's tools, vehichles (land), vehicles (water), and woodcarver's tools.

1st Level: Bonus Cantrip

Additionally you know the druidcraft cantrip.

2nd Level: Channel Divinity: Shared Bounty

Starting at 2nd level, you can use your channel divinity to perform minor miracles in the name of your faith or deity. As an action you can expend your channel divinity to do one of the following:

Bless This Feast. You bless a meal for up to a number of creatures equal to your wisdom modifier. Anyone who partakes of the meal in the next hour gains a number of temporary hit points equal to your cleric level times two.

Increase Bounty. You take a single mundane meal and duplicate it a number of times equal to your wisdom modifier.

Resilient Harvest. You lay a blessing on an area of arable fertile land that is being or will be used for crops. The area can be no larger than your cleric level times 20 square feet. This patch of land will become resistant to infestation, rot, decay, and any other spoil for 1 full season. (It is up to the DM how this exactly is worked out but it should give the equivelant of resistance to poison damage and advantage on saving throws against relevant effects).

6th Level: Godly Cook

At 6th level, when you cook a morsel made with grown and harvested ingredients you can expend a spell slot. When a creature eats that food (as an action on their turn with the 'use an object' action for instance) within the next 24 hours, they are healed for 1d6 +your wisdom modifier hit points. The amount healed is increased by another 1 d6 for every level above 1st the spell slot expended was.

After 24 hours the food loses its potency but is still edible.

8th Level: Hew the Line

Starting at 8th level, you have mastered your talent with reaping harvests and learned to bring this talent to the battlefield. Onceo n each of your turns when you hit a creature with a melee weapon attack, you deal 1d8 radiant damage to another creature of your choice within 10 feet of the attack's target. When you reach 14th level, this damage increases to 2d8.

17th Level: Mighty Meal

At 17th level, you know the spell heroes' feast and it is considered a domain spell for you (so you always have it prepared). You can cast heroes' feast with its casting time reduced to 1 minute without any material components. Once you cast the spell in this manner you must complete a long rest before you can do so again.

Fluff

Where the domains of nature and life meet the Harvest is born. Those clerics who devote themselves to the Harvest are givers: always willing to aid those in need of a meal. The Harvest is bountiful and gives to all in turn.
