Geographical Nomenclature

Greyhawk is the name of one of the major cities in the campaign setting.

Oerth is the name of the planet on which the City of Greyhawk can be found. There are four continents. Oerik is the name of the continent on which the City of Greyhawk can be found.

The Flanaess is the easternmost portion of Oerik. It can be thought of as analogous to Europe’s position on the Eurasian continent (although on the opposite end, geographically speaking). The Flanaess is considered the “enlightened center” of culture on Oerth, especially by those who dwell in the Flanaess.

This campaign will not be taking place - or at least not starting - in Greyhawk. Instead it will be starting in the Barony of Reyifor (Barony of Reyifor Player Map) in the realm of Sterich when your characters arrive at Frandor's Keep.

  • Help Action (Variant): When you use the Help action to aid another character in a task, you must have proficiency in that Skill and make a DC 10 skill check with that skill. You cannot Help if you suffer disadvantage to the roll (such as from Exhaustion)
  • Human variant not available.
  • Inventory Bulk Rules (Modified) (modified - 2/11/2025)
  • Limited Magic Item Market (new - 2/2/2025)
  • Milestone Leveling
  • Sleeping in Armor: Sleeping in light armor has no adverse effect on the wearer, but sleeping in medium or heavy armor makes it difficult to recover fully during a long rest. When you finish a long rest during which you slept in medium or heavy armor, you regain only one quarter of your spent Hit Dice (minimum of one die). If you have any levels of exhaustion, the rest doesn’t reduce your exhaustion level.
