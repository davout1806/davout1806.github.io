1st Level: Bonus Proficiencies or Spells

You must choose if you are a Waxingmoon or a Waningmoon.

Waxingmoons: Gain proficiency with all martial melee weapons and heavy armor.

Waningmoons: You gain either the Deepen Shadows #EFA# or Light cantrip if you don't already know it. It does not count towards your Cantrips known. You also gain either Sanctuary or Faerie Fire if you don't already know it. The spell is always prepared and counts as one of your domain spells.

1st Level: Moonsight

When under the light of the moon you have darkvision 60 ft, but not underground, in buildings, or otherwise cut off from moonlight.

2nd Level: Channel Divinity: Moonbeam

At 2nd level you can use your channel divinity as a full action to choose a 30 foot area you can see. The silver light of the moon shines in that radius. When you cast it, choose one of the effects:

  • All creatures inside take 1d6 radiant damage whenever they enter the beam or end their turn inside. If you are a Waxingmoon this becomes 1d8.
  • All creatures inside heal for 1d6 hit points whenever they enter the beam or end their turn inside. If you are a Waningmoon this becomes 1d8.

6th Level: Channel Divinity: Moonlight Cleanse

Starting at 6th level, you can use your Channel Divinity to dispel enchantments and illusions. You can use your action to choose any creature, object, or magical effect within 60 feet. For each spell effect, you make an ability check using your spellcasting ability. The DC equals 10 + the spell’s level. On a successful check, the spell ends. This feature only applies to spells from the enchantment and illusion schools.

8th Level: Shared Healing

From 8th level, whenever you heal an ally with a spell, you heal for half of that healing (rounded down).

17th Level: Moonfire Transcendance

At 17th level, you channel moonlight into your eyes, turning them into fiery wisps. You can use action to activate an aura of moonfire that lasts for 1 minute or until you dismiss it using another action. Your vision is treated as if having Darkvision up to 60 feet.

You emit bright light in a 60-foot radius and dim light 30 feet beyond that. As an action, you may choose a hostile creature you can see within the bright light and make a ranged spell attack. On a hit, the creature takes radiant damage equal to 2d10 + your cleric level. If the creature has the shapechanger subtype, it takes an additional 2d10 radiant damage. Alternatively, you may choose a hostile creature you can see within the dim light and make a ranged spell attack. On a hit, the creature takes radiant damage equal to 1d10 + half your cleric level. If the creature has the shapechanger subtype, it takes an additional 1d10 radiant damage.

Enchantment and illusions spells of 3rd-level or lower automatically fail when cast within your light. Additionally, you and allied creatures within your light have advantage on saving throws against enchantment and illusion spells.

Fluff

Clerics of the moon preach that there is a light in every night that guides out way. They are also keepers of great libraries of ancient and forbidden knowledge. The priest of this Domain are split into two groups known as the Waxingmoons and the Waningmoons. They have different training, Waxingmoons train in weapons and armor while Waningmoons train harder in spellcasting.
