(moo-AM-man doo-AH-thuhl)

Muamman is the protector of adventurers, travelers, expatriates in non-dwarven or mixed communities, and all those dwarves who travel or live far from the dwarven homelands. His is a growing cult, and he may be evolving into an intermediate god. His secondary aspect is unique among dwarves: as a god of lightning, which he often uses as an omen.

The theme of traveling to gain knowledge is a concern of this god. Muamman sometimes visits the Prime Material Plane to act as a guide or to warn urban dwarves of trouble brewing in the homelands. Muamman appears as a weathered dwarf of nondescript appearance, dressed very plainly.

Muamman's clerics establish way-places along roads and very long tunnel complexes, and outdoors they maintain highways, mountain trails, and passes. The priesthood is a strong wandering and adventuring one. The clerics also have a significant role in many urban, expatriate dwarven communities. Full priests (level 3 and up) are known as Watchful Eyes.
