1st Level: Bonus Proficiencies

At 1st level, you gain proficiency in Intimidation. If you are already proficient, you gain Expertise in it instead, applying double your Proficiency bonus to Intimidation skill checks.

1st Level: Retributive Flame

At 1st level, you are gifted with the ability to punish the wicked. You gain the sacred flame cantrip as a bonus cantrip. When you use the Attack action, you can cast sacred flame as a bonus action.

You can use this feature a number of times equal to your Wisdom modifier (a minimum of once). You regain all expended uses when you finish a long rest.

2nd Level: Channel Divinity: Vow of Enmity

Beginning at 2nd level, as a bonus action you can use your Channel Divinity to utter a vow of enmity against a creature you can see within 10 feet of you. You gain advantage on attack rolls against the creature for 1 minute or until it drops to 0 hit points or falls unconscious.

6th Level: Unwavering Drive

Beginning at 6th level you gain immunity to charmed and frightened conditions.

8th Level: Retributive Strike

Starting at 8th level, you can smite foes with a retributive strike. When a creature that is within 5 feet of you makes an attack on you or an ally, you can use your reaction to make a melee attack against that creature. This melee attack deals an extra 1d8 psychic damage and the creature must make a Wisdom saving throw or be frightened until the end of your next turn. When you reach 14th level, the extra damage increases to 2d8.

17th Level: Vengeful Visions

At 17th level, when you hit a creature with an attack, you can use this feature to instantly cause the target to relive terrifying phantasmal visions of their past deeds. The creature disappears and hurtles through a nightmarish landscape within the Shadowfell.

At the end of your next turn, the target returns to the space it previously occupied, or the nearest unoccupied space and takes 10d10 psychic damage as it reels from its horrific experience.

Once you use this feature, you can't use it again until you finish a long rest.
