(BAYR-ah-vahr KLOHK-sha-doh)

Baravar is a sneaky, sly gnomish deity. While he is a protector of the gnomish race, his protections are actively rooted in deceit - illusions, traps, ambushes, and the like. His jests and tricks may cause no little discomfort to the victims. Baravar has a genuine antipathy for deities of many goblinoid races and, unlike the other gnomish gods, he is none too restrained about expressing this. He is also a thief, albeit not a very good one, and enjoys using illusions to confuse creatures before robbing them. Often, he steals out of boredom as much as for any other reason.

Baravar's clerics must adventure and conduct magical experiments to find magical items controlling illusions. Many are cleric/illusionists. They are sneaky, smart gnomes and have a role as spies and investigative agents.
