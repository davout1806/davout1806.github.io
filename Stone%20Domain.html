1st Level: Rock Training

When you choose this domain at first level, you gain proficiency with heavy armor and the flail, maul, morningstar, and warhammer.

Additionally, when you would add your proficiency bonus to Wisdom or Intelligence checks to know about rock and elemental plane of earth, or ability checks using earth-based artisan’s tools (such as glassblower’s, jeweler’s, mason’s, potter’s, or smith’s tools) or vehicles (land) you instead add double your proficiency bonus.

1st Level: Stone Magic

At first level, you know the Mold Earth cantrip. It does not count against your total number of cantrips known.

2nd Level: Channel Divinity: Stability

At 2nd level while in contact with stone or earth, you can expend your Channel Divinity as a reaction when you make a saving throw to gain advantage on that saving throw.

If an ally within 10 feet of you and in contact with stone or earth and makes a saving throw, you can expend your Channel Divinity as a reaction to grant that ally advantage on the saving throw.

6th Level: Strength of Stone

At 6th level, you can expend your Channel Divinity after you hit an enemy to deal extra bludgeoning damage equal to twice your cleric level and shove the target. If either you or the target is in contact with stone or earth, you deal an extra 1d8 bludgeoning damage.

8th Level: Divine Strike

At 8th level, you gain the ability to infuse your weapon strikes with divine energy. Once on each of your turns when you hit a creature with a weapon attack, you can cause the attack to deal an extra 1d8 bludgeoning damage to the target. When you reach 14th level, the extra damage increases to 2d8.

17th Level: Stone’s Chosen

At 17th level, you have resistance to non-magical bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage.

Additionally, you cannot be forcibly moved. Attempts to shove you, knock you prone, or otherwise move you automatically fail unless the creature possesses legendary actions, in which case you have advantage on all skill checks and saving throws to resist forcible movement.
