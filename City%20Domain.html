1st Level: Bonus Cantrip

When you choose this domain at 1st level, you gain the Mending cantrip.

1st Level: Bonus Proficencies

Also starting at 1st level, you gain proficiency with Masons Tools and Carpenters Tools and you learn one extra language of your choice.

1st Level: Heart of the City

From 1st level, you are able to tap into the spirit of community found in the city. While you are within any city, you can gain advantage on a single Charisma (Deception, Intimidation, or Persuasion check), and you are considered proficient in the appropriate skill. You can use this feature a number of times equal to your Wisdom modifier (a minimum of once. You regain any expended uses when you finish a long rest.

2nd Level: Spirits of the City

Starting at 2nd level, you can use your Channel Divinity to call on the city for aid. As an action, you present your holy symbol, each hostile creature within 30 feet of you must make a Charisma saving throw. On a failed save, the creature is knocked prone or restrained (your choice by hazards such as roads collapsing to unseen potholes, doors slamming into enemies, roofs collapsing, and so on.) A restrained creature can escape by making a successful Strength (Athletics) or Dexterity (Acrobatics) check against your spell save DC.

6th Level: Block Watch

Starting at 6th level, your awareness while in the city extends preternaturally. While in an urban environment, you are considered proficient in the Insight and Perception skills, and you add double your proficiency bonus to Wisdom (Insight and Wisdom (Perception) checks, instead of your normal proficiency bonus.

8th Level: Divine Strike

At 8th level, you gain the ability to infuse your weapon strikes with spiritual energy borrowed from the citizens of your city. Once on each of your turns when you hit a creature with a weapon attack, you can cause the attack to deal an extra 1d8 psychic damage to the target. When you reach 14th level, the extra psychic damage increases to 2d8.

17th Level: Bloodlines of the City

At 17th level, you can use trading areas to transport you and up to 5 other willing creatures, of size medium or smaller, instantaneously to another trading area in another city you have been to before. Starting from a shop, a warehouse, or a guildhall, you can teleport to the center of the merchant district or any other similar trading area in a city you have been to before, as if you had cast a teleport spell whose destination is a permanent teleportation circle you know. Once you use this feature, you must finish a long rest before using it again.

Fluff

The City domain is concerned with the citizenry, commerce, traffic, and even architecture of modern civilization. In the eyes of a cleric of the city, the center of life is a sense and spirit of community, and the gravest enemies of the city are those who seek to harm the common weal of its citizens.
