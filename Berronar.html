(BE-roh-nahr)

The greatest of all dwarf goddesses is usually held to be Berronar, who lives with Moradin in the Seven Heavens at the Soul Forge beneath the mountains. Berronar is seen as the patron of marriage and love (but not necessarily romance). Her name is often invoked in small home rituals, for protection from thieves and duplicity. However, Berronar is not a passive homebody; her efforts to preserve and protect dwarf culture and civilization have spanned many universes, and dwarf of both sexes revere her name.

Berronar appears to be a huge dwarf wearing chain mail of bright silver. Her brown beard is braided into four rows. If a braid is cut off, it will regrow in a single day, and at the end of that day, the lock of hair that was cut off will turn to gold. She sometimes gives locks of her hair to dwarf communities that are exceptionally poor or hard-pressed and otherwise unable to get on their feet economically. This is an extremely rare occurrence, of course, and must be invoked by a lawful good dwarf cleric of the highest level in the colony. If this gold or any part of it is used for other than good purposes, it will all disappear. Berronar wields a mace of gold and steel that will slay evil thieves and all assassins on contact. She wears two silver rings of great power; one prevents anyone from knowingly telling a falsehood within her presence, and the other prevents the use of all thieving abilities by any mortals within the same area.

The dwarf have a custom of exchanging rings with those for whom they feel exceptional, mutual trust and love; this independently parallels a human custom used in marriage rituals. This dwarf ceremony is not lightly taken and is almost never undertaken with a non-dwarf except in the most unusual circumstances. Both participants must be lawful good. Berronar is said to have inspired this practice, and several variations of it exist around the dwarf realms.

If a lawful good dwarf fighter appeals to Berronar for aid for a particular purpose and makes an appropriately large sacrifice, there is a chance she will bestow upon the individual the powers of a paladin of equal level for several days. Only dwarf of exceptionally pure heart will be considered for this honor, and it may be granted only once every ten years to any individual.

Berronar's clerics are guardians and protectors of dwarf clans and maintain lore-records and family histories. They must also heal sick or injured dwarf and those in need. Clerics of Berronar cast the following spells as if they were four levels higher: endure cold/heat, protection from evil (or 10Ê radius), resist cold/fire, sanctuary, withdraw, dispel magic, negative plane protection, remove paralysis, cause paralysis, spell immunity, dispel evil, and forbiddance. Clerical level titles are as follows: Daughter of Berronar, Hearth Mistress, Homesteader, Lore Keeper, Faith Keeper, Fidelite, Sacred Heart, Revered Sister, and Keeper of the Truesilver.
