6th Level: Channel Divinity: Intoxicating Presence

At 6th level you can use your action to create an aura of wild festivity. You present your holy symbol forcing all creatures within 15 feet of you to make a Wisdom saving throw.

Each creature that fails its saving throw must use half its movement speed to dance merrily in a circle centered on you.It cannot use its remaining movement, take actions, bonusactions, or reactions. Its wild flailing grants it advantage on Dexterity saving throws.

Affected creatures can repeat their Wisdom saving throwat the start of each of their turns, ending the spell on asuccess. The spell also ends for an affected creature if it takes damage, or after 1 minute has passed

8th Level: Divine Strike

You gain the ability to infuse your weapon strikes with divine energy. Once on each of your turns when you hit a creature with a weapon attack, you can cause the attack to deal an extra 1d8 radiant damage to the target. When you reach 14th level, the extra damage increases to 2d8.

17th Level: Carnival Eternal

Starting at 17th leve the range of your Intoxicating Presence feature is extended to 30 feet, and you can choose which creatures are affected by it. Additionally, you can choose to fill the space with festive music, hypnotic lights, and shadowy dancing figures, creating the following effects for the duration (1 minute):

  • the area is considered difficult terrain for creatures other than you
  • creatures in the area have disadvantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks
  • a creature attempting to cast or concentrate on a spell in the area must make a concentration check against your spell save DC at the start of its turn. On a failure, the spell fails and its slot is consumed.
