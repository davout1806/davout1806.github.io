Story Points

 11th February 2025 at 12:17pm
Campaign: GreyhawkDnD RulePublicRule Variant

Story Points can be spent on 2 things:

  1. For 100 points you get 1 Inspiration that lasts until you use it. And they can stack up to 5.
  2. For 50 points you get 1 randomly drawn Drama Card. You can potentially keep these Drama Cards between sessions. At the end of each session, roll a d20 for each unused Drama Card. If the d20 is equal to or lower than the value listed in the table below for the type, you lose the card to cold heartless GM.
