PublicSession Notes

Our Heros

  • Delvan Brightwood – Half-elf 2nd level Paladin
  • Flint Sunsong – Dwarf 2nd level Monk
  • Gwador – Half-orc 2nd level Fighter

Planting 11th, 576 CY

The Old Mine

  • Went further into Old Mine
  • Encountered the odor of rotten eggs, possibly from cracks in rocks.
  • Encountered and subdued 2 bandits who had got into an altercation with some Rikar’s Ravens and ended up killing them. They came to the mine to hide out. Assumed the party were sent by Rikar. The party tied up the bandits, took their coins and scimitars, and broke their crossbows.
  • Found a corroded winch & pulley mechanism with rope going down lower level of mine.
    • Also in the room was two beaver pelts, a brass signal mirror, a bloody set of thieves tools, a miner’s lantern, and four mining picks.
      • Two of the picks were given to bandits above.
      • Gwador took one pick and left it by the old hollow tree in the woods outside the keep.
      • Flint took the thieves tools.
      • Delvan took the lantern.
  • Repelled down rope found natural cavern with mining tools rusted into walls.
  • The cavern led to looping & twisting tunnels. They came to an intersection where they heard a haunting whisper say "I was not strong enough… the earth does not forgive the unworthy."
  • Came to cavern shored up with timber. Occasionally the shiny glint of metal could be seen in the walls. Water slowly driped from the ceiling and the floor wads covered by about an inch of murky fluid.
  • On the far wall was an inscription
    • "Strength is not in steel, but in the will to endure. The Earth-Forged shall stand Unbent." – Draeligor
    • Flint recognized the name Draeligor as someone who was previously sent on the same quest as him.
    • Flint also noticed that inscription was still crisp and undamaged - likely within last 120 years.
  • While Flint and Delvan were inspecting the inscription and Gwador was looking at the metal deposits, They noticed a skeleton with purple spectral clouds swirls around it had appeared behind it. Flint tried to communicate. Gwador approached, the skeleton fired spectral darts from its hands towards Gwador. Combat ensued, eventually the party was victorious.
  • Moved through a series of other tunnels to other intersections, where they heard other whispers:
    • "The unbent fell. The broken rose."
    • "He knelt before the trial… but his faith faltered."
    • "The bracer was his… and yet it was not his to wield."
  • At this point two heat emitting skeletons came out from an alcove and attacked.
  • At one more intersection they heard a final whisper, "Return what was lost… or let it remain cursed forever."
  • Went back to the room with inscription, Flint touched it, he had a vision of a warrior kneeling before a trial, his fists wrapped in ancient bracers, whispering. The warrior was facing a challenge vs some sort earth being.
  • Gwador took some some of the metal hoping it was gold. Flint informed him it was iron pyrite.
  • Returned to the bandits.
    • Delvan asked them if they had learned their lesson. Which they replied they had.
    • The party not being friends with Rikar, not wanting to turn the bandits over to Rikar.
    • Delvan showed the bandits the fools gold, told them it was gold the party had found in the mine that the bandits could excavate. Or they were free to leave. Or Delvan suggested they could offer Rikar info about the "gold" in the mine as a peace offering.
    • One of the bandits asked how are they to acquire food if there was a bear at the entrance?
      • The party led the bandits to the bear and with hand gestures tried to impress onto the bear that the bandits were with the party.
      • Whether the bear really understood the message remains to be seen.
    • What bandits decided to do remained to be seen.

Back to the Keep

  • One way back to Keep they note old tree with hollow (they had been keeping eye out for such a tree in that area) - in referrence to Rumor .
  • Sold beaver pelts for 3 gp each
  • Since, they weren't going to get any money for them, they donated scimitars to the keep
  • At the Broken Hilt Tavern
    • Grond came up the party and "I have to apologize when you first came to me I got so upset about the message board I completely forgot about your related question. A week back the caravan I was with was attacked by a group of wolf-men.". The party agreed to Grond joining them to journey to location of the attack.
    • Lamar Vikara came up to the party, long story short: “Seems some conscripts got a round of milk. Poor lads, too proud to laugh it off, too riled up to let it go. A joke? Maybe. A stupid one? Definitely. But you know what I won’t allow? A soldier turning on a soldier under my roof. This place? It’s a wild one. But I don’t let it turn into a battlefield. So if there’s someone out there thinking about stirring the pot, maybe trying to see how far they can push things—best they think twice. ‘Cause I clean up my own messes.”
    • Lamar then adds "Heard you fellas been asking questions. You know, coin and drinks tend lossen lipps."
