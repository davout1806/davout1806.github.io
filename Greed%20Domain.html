1st Level: Tithes

You gain the ability to impose a debt upon those who call on your services. Whenever you cast a level one or higher spell that benefits a creature other than yourself, they can choose to become Indebted to you, a unique condition that lasts until that creature completes a short rest.

While indebted, a creature gains additional temporary hit points equal to your Wisdom modifier whenever they are subject to a beneficial spell you cast of level one or higher.

As a bonus action on your turn, you can choose to remove the Indebted condition on one creature within 60 feet of you to grant yourself a bonus to attack and damage rolls equal to your Charisma modifier on weapon attacks and single target spell attacks. If you do so, the creature who was indebted to you makes attack rolls and damage rolls on their next turn at disadvantage.

1st Level: Wealth

You have an accumulation of Wealth, a point pool that you can uses to empower your other features in this class. Your pool equals your Wisdom modifier, minimum 1. You regain all spent Wealth points when you complete a long rest.

As a bonus action, you can do the following involving your Wealth:

  • You can spend a point of Wealth to increase the damage you deal with a Cleric Cantrip by your Wisdom modifier.
  • You can spend two points of Wealth to regain one use of your Channel Divinity Feature. This additional use can only be used on Channel Divinities granted by this Domain.
  • You can spend a point of Wealth to grant yourself a +1 bonus to AC until the start of your next turn.
  • You can spend a point of Wealth to allow yourself to make one weapon attack.

You can elect to remove the indebted condition on a creature in order to grant yourself a point of Wealth. You can do this a number of times equal to your Charisma Modifier, minimum one. You regain the use of this ability after the completion of a short rest. Additionally, whenever you gain Wealth, you regain hit points equal to your Wisdom modifier.

2nd Level: Channel Divinity: Paid Protection

As a reaction when a friendly creature adjacent to you is hit by an attack, you can instead take their place for the damage dealt. If the creature allows, whatever damage they would have taken from the attack, you instead take, of the same damage type. After you use this feature, that creature is considered indebted to you for the purposes of Tithes. If the creature is already Indebted to you, you gain a point of Wealth, and they make attack and damage rolls at disadvantage on their next turn.

2nd Level: Channel Divinity: Extortion

You can choose a creature within 60 feet of you. That creature makes a Wisdom saving throw. On a failed save, the target is stunned for up to 1 minute. At the end of each of its turns, the target can elect to make another Wisdom saving throw. On a success, the creature is no longer stunned. The target can elect to become Indebted to you in order to immediately save against the effect.

6th Level: Paid Indifference

As a reaction when you are hit by an attack, you can instead elect to pay off the enemy. The attack is instead made at disadvantage. Using this feature expends a Wealth Point.

8th Level: Divine Strike

You gain the ability to infuse your weapon strikes with divine energy. Once on each of your turns when you hit a creature with a weapon attack, you can cause the attack to deal an extra 1d8 damage of the same type dealt by the weapon to the target. When you reach 14th level, the extra damage increases to 2d8.

17th Level: Wealth is Power

Your attacks benefit from your stored wealth. You gain a bonus to your attack rolls equal to the amount of your stored Wealth.

Fluff

Greed Domain clerics are entirely concerned with the ceremony of the church. They perform ceremonies and aid those who provide their deities with tithings. They pull tithes from their followers and use them to gain benefits from their deity. They often are seen in a negative light, uncaring for generosity, but their single focus on value leads to some extraordinary effects.
