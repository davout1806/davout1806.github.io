1st Level: Swiftness

When you choose this domain at 1st level your movement speed is increased by 10 feet.

1st Level: Deft Acrobat

When you choose this domain at 1st level when you select this domain you gain proficiency in Acrobatics.

2nd Level: Channel Divinity: Dance of the Wind

Starting at 2nd level you can use your Channel Divinity to aid you and your allies, or hinder your foes, with powerful winds that surround you 60 feet in every direction. When you use this ability you choose to either increase your allies speed by 10 feet or reduce your enemies speed by 10 feet. If you aid your allies they gain advantage on dexterity saving throws, if you hinder your enemies they have disadvantage on dexterity saving throws.

6th Level: Swift Striker

Starting at 6th level if you move 25 feet before making a melee weapon or melee spell attack you gain advantage on the attack roll.

You can use this feature a number of times equal to your Wisdom modifier (a minimum of once). You regain all expended uses when you finish a long rest.

8th Level: Divine Strike

Starting at 8th level you gain the ability to infuse your weapon strikes with divine energy. Once on each of your turns when you hit a creature with a weapon attack, you can cause the attack to deal an extra 1d8 slashing damage. When you reach 14th level the extra damage increases to 2d8.

17th Level: Graceful Winds

Starting at 17th level you gain a flight speed equal to your walking speed. While flying you can use a bonus action to take the Dash or Disengage action.

Fluff

Clerics of the Wind domain focus on swift attacks and graceful dodging. Using the winds around them they weave between foes with ease, delivering lightning fast blows to end the fight in a single attack.
