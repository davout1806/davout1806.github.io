Ice Bow #ECD#

 14th March 2026 at 3:51pm
DB GeneratedDnD RulePublicSpell

2nd-level evocation

Casting Time1 Bonus Action
RangeSelf.
ComponentsV, S, M (A piece of Sapphire worth at least 20 gold pieces)
DurationUp to 10 Minute.

You create a curved bow made of ice in your hand, similar in size to a shortbow, that lasts for the duration. If you let go of the bow it shatters to pieces but can be resummoned using a bonus action. You can use an action to make a ranged spell attack with the bow. On a hit the target takes 2d8 cold damage.

At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for every two slot levels above 2nd.
