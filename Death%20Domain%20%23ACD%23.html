1st Level: Death Sight

At first level you gain the ability to gauge the health of any being. If you concentrate for a moment (or use an action in combat), you know the health of a number of beings up to your Cleric level. Health includes hit points remaining, any diseases present (normal or magical), and any poisons or other harmful substances they are suffering from.

1st Level: Bonus Proficiency

When you choose this domain at first level, you gain proficiency with the Stealth skill.

1st Level: Darkness of the Grave

Also at first level, you can cloak one being in darkness. The space is equivalent to a medium sized creature. The cloaked creature cannot see or hear. The effect lasts as long as you concentrate on that creature. Any light spell removes this effect. At 9th level you can cloak a Large creature. And at 15th level you can cloak a Huge creature.

2nd Level: Channel Divinity: Touch of Death

Starting at 2nd level you can use your Channel Divinity to cause harm directly to others. As an action, you reach out and touch another living being. Your touch causes 2d8 damage + your cleric level.

6th Level: Stay in Your Grave

At 6th level, you gain improved ability to destroy or deal damage to the Undead – those that would cheat your deity out of the souls rightfully theirs. Cleric level to destroy undead is two lower than normal. For example CR 1 undead are destroyed at 6th level. And if the undead makes its saving throw, it takes 2d8 damage instead.

8th Level: Aura of Death

At 8th level, add Proficiency with Intimidation based on your Wisdom modifier, not Charisma. Also, Beings that you kill may not be raised as Undead.

17th Level: Undead Hunter

Starting at 17th level, your range for turning undead increases to 60 feet and damage from a failed save increases to 4d8.

Fluff

Death is reviled, feared, and universally misunderstood. Deities claiming influence over Death are rejected by most cities and have their temples banned. Clerics of Death normally do not like to advertise their profession, often staying hidden to avoid the fear almost all beings have for death and the unknown.
