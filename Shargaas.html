(SHAHR-gahs)

Shargaas the Night Lord lives in a tremendous cavern system below the fiery plain of one of the levels of Gehenna. It is said that his caves extend infinitely and are darker than the blackest night. There, no creature has sight but Shargaas himself and his orcs spirit servants. Shargaas, though blinded completely by light from the sun, can see perfectly well in darkness out to a range of a mile or more. He can also climb any surface, even perfectly smooth ones, without slipping. In the days when he is said to have walked upon the Oerth, Shargaas could also hide himself and his followers so well that no mortal could detect his ambushes or lairs. As might be expected, orc bandits and half-orc thieves hold Shargaas as their patron, as do other regular orc clans. The major religious holidays in the worship of Shargaas are the times of the new moon, when the sky is dark and cloudy.

Shargaas' clerics are thieves and assassins, sneaky killers who practice their skills continually. They are scouts and advance spies for armies, but only operate in darkness. Shargaas' own clans are always underground dwellers. Acolytes of this cult (levels 182) are called Night Shadows; full priests (level 3+) are Dark Lords. The highest-level priest of any clan is called the Deep Dark.
