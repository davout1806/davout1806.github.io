Our Heros

  • Delvon Brightwood – Half-elf Paladin
  • Flint Sunsong – Dwarf Monk
  • Gimblebok – Gnome Druid
  • Gwador – Half-orc Fighter

How Our Heroes Met

  • Each individually traveled along the Boarderland Road through the villages of Sabden and Vew.
  • Reached Frandor’s Keep on 8th of Planting.
  • While at the Palisade outside of Frandor’s Keep, Gimblebok noticed a caged mountain lion. Inquiring with the guards who informed him it was the result of Jiric Helsan's bounty, who pays money for people to capture animals and humanoids alive for gladiatorial games in the Hold of the Sea Princes.
  • While Delvon Brightwood headed toward Quarrytown outside of Frandor’s Keep, Shanko approached and asked if Delvon could spare a few coin for a man down on his luck. Delvon gave him 1 gp. "May the gods bless you. Now be careful aroud here, especially at night. Especially at night. I've seen things" - “I saw my buddy Thak ripped to threads by a man-wolf in Vell's Clearing”
  • While Flint Sunsong waited in line to get into Frandor’s Keep, the boy Vic approached and asked if he needed a guided. Flint declined but maybe tomorrow.
  • A scene broke out in line ahead of Flint. Flint could hear and see this. Delvon and Gimblebok could only see.
    • A half-orc (who later it is revealed to be Gwador) was attempting to enter the keep which caused everyone around to stop what they were doing, causing a silence. This led to the attraction of the keep guards and this scene.
    • Guard 1: Sword pointed at chest of Gwador "Where do you think you're going mucksnout? You think you can waltz in like some common traveler?"
    • Guard 2: "You’re lucky we ain’t still stacking your kind’s skulls along the wall."
    • Gwador: "has to eat. I need work."
    • Guard 1: (Scoffing.) “Oh, so you speak the tongue of men. Pity. Might’ve been easier if you just growled and showed your teeth like the rest.”
    • Guard 2 to Guard 1: "Maybe he's Rikar's merc"
    • Guard 1 to Guard 2: "If he's Rikar's then why he's here looking for work?"
    • Guard 2 to Guard 1: "Well, I don't know. Maybe he already got sick of working for the man. How many mucksnouts do you think are coming around here?"
    • Gwador: "I'm not mucksnout"
    • Guard 1: "Oh you're 100% prime grade mucksnoot!"
    • At this point Delvon approached in case it became violent
    • Gimblebok walked towards the keep’s gate
    • Gwador, pointing to Gimblebok, informed the guards that person was his boss – even though they never met.
    • In the end this flummoxed the guards who told the two of them to follow the rules and check in with the Office of the Keep.
  • At this point Flint and Delvon joined Gwador and Gimblebok.

The Party has Formed and Enters the Keep

  • Upon entering the keep, they met Randric who offered his services as a local guide, which the group agreed.
  • Group visited the Offices of the Keep where they had to surrender their medium or larger melee weapons and all ranged weapons. Also informed that the keep’s gates close at dusk and do not open for any reason until dawn.
  • Randric Told them the following bits news and gossip:
    • The statue at the Golden Lynx Gate has been stolen
    • “There’s something strange going on out at Karapar Tower, I tell ya. Soldiers are actin’ real strange — hauling equipment up them foot trails, and bein’ real secretive. I’ve heard talk they may have struck silver out there and are keeping it on the lowdown.“
    • “Dark spirits of dead ghosts and orc gods demand appeasement from travelers on the Hell's Throat Trail. Appeasing these spirits requires the gift of gold placed in the hollow of the old tree northwest of the Keep.”
    • “You know Ealon Pifor’s wife, that cripple out at Kar Darkan waystation? A giant spooked her horse, and it threw her. That’s why she’s crippled.”
    • “One of the sergeants out at Dartan Tower has a reg’lar visit from Dela of the Broken Hilt Tavern. I hear she gives him free services, if ’n ya know what I mean. Wink. Wink.”
    • “Two goblins captured by bounty hunters for Jiric slipped their shackles last week. Had half the garrison chasing them through the compound before they were put down.”
    • “The guard unit of the Great Tower are being punished for failing to notice vandals who used stones to spell a slur to the baron under his mansion under in Renar's Lot.”
    • “The Keep is encircled by several dozen sets of wolf tracks. An expert tracker noted that some of the wolves may not be wolves at all, but are of worgs.“
  • Group saw the public message board
  • Talked with Kiparus “Kip” Belan at Bounty Master about the 1gp per pair of ear of humanoids bounty established by the Baron.
  • Visited Broken Hilt Tavern
    • Talked to Grond
      • Found out he’s a mercenary looking for work
      • Found out he hired scribe to post about looking for said work not dwarves looking for “tunneling” activities. Grond was understandably enraged when informed what note actually said. Delvon and Grond confonted Lakar Vitisar, the scribe Grond had hired to write the post. Lakar was forced to rectify the problem.
  • Inquired about job regarding Vespin tower at the Inner Gate told to speak to scribe Dilan Vitisar, who by the way was Lakar’s cousin.
    • Dilan informed group:
      • that military had lost contact with Vespin tower
      • needed someone to go there, investigate, and report what happened
      • military at keep does not report to Baron but to Earl of Sterich through the Prefect of the Keep
  • Left the keep, travelled along the Hell’s Throat Trail on the way to Vespin Tower.
  • Vell's Clearing is on the way to Vespin Tower. It’s just an area along Hell’s Throat Trail that is clear of vegetation except for some thorny wax berry bushes.
  • Further along the trail the group was ambushed by lone bugbear. While deadly from its ambush, numbers overwhelmed it. Delvon claimed it’s morningstar.
  • At Vespin Tower
    • Found the splattered bodies of two dead goblins, presumed fallen from the tower or climbing to/from the tower.
    • After reaching the top of the tower group discovered tower occupied by 3 goblins, 2 of whom were asleep. The group despatched quickly of the goblins. Also in the keep were corpses of 2 guards.
    • Treasure: 12 torches, 13 sp, 2 spears. I should have been clear the silver pieces were on the goblins not the guards.

He was expecting to have those supplies and therefore planned ahead to schedule security to for trip to Kar Mandrii.
