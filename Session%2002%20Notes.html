PublicSession Notes

Our Heros

  • Delvon Brightwood – Half-elf Paladin
  • Flint Sunsong – Dwarf Monk
  • Gimblebok – Gnome Druid
  • Gwador – Half-orc Fighter

Planting 10th, 576 CY

Woman In Distress

  • Woman claiming to be Leona Brast asked for the party's help. He said her husband had been missing since yesterday when he went to meet a merchant about a debt. He was reportly last seen on the Old Has Road near the intersection with The Boarderland Road.
  • During the journey, the party was ambushed by 5 thugs who told them than that Jiric Helsan had a message that "Helsan says you need a lesson about minding your own damn business."
  • With the aid of someone who did not reveal himself until the fighting was over, the party was able to win the fight. Killing 3 and capturing 2.
  • Their unknown ally was a Priest of Trithereon by the name of Goazaar, who said:
    • he had a run in with Jiric before
    • Randric has been missing since last night. Last seen at the graveyard.
    • Asked for the party's help freeing some slave prostitutes from the Den of Delights in Quarrytown.
    • Rikar pretty much runs Quarrytown.
  • They took all their captives' gear and clothes, tied their hands hands their backs and allowed them to go where they pleased - they went back to the Keep.

Searching for Randric

  • Party returned to the Keep, went to the graveyard
    • Saw list of names of Knight-Commanders on wall of Keep.
    • Met Shajani, who talked about undead being around at night.
    • Discovered the abandoned chapel had been bricked up.
    • Discovered foot prints between a grave - that was unearthed - and a tunnel.
    • Going into the tunnel lead to a small room like area just under Keep where they found:
      • a ghoul. What they later found out about the ghoul before he was a ghoul:
        • Vos Wilkers
        • Soldier, likely of some connection since he was buried rather than cremated.
        • Died at the age of 25
      • the dead body of Randric
      • a skull with two missing front teeth
      • an iron ring (engraved with 'to my love Evon')
      • a carved wooden flute
      • four platinum coins - which had their letters and image backwards
      • and a lockbox that contained a letter claiming a Sir Aedric Vane was no traitor
  • The list of names of Knight-Commanders on wall of Keep has one name chisled out. The Party assumes that name is Aedric Vane.
  • Flint went to the scribes, asked about a sign with the name Sir Aedric Vane of the right size that would fit on the list Knight-Commanders list over the chisled out part.
    • Was told 12 gp. Flint replied never mind.
  • Gawdor attempted to sell breastplate at Anari's Arms, was told to leave.
  • Flint was able to sell the breastplate for 40 gp.
  • Returned Randric's body to authorities
  • Informed Randric's mother of his death, returned the money he earned from the party to her.
  • Delvan & Gimblebok went to Carpenter too ask about Leona Brast.
  • There they met master carpenter Mal Varasin and his journyman Feranis. They had never heard of Leona Brast.

Welcome to Quarrytown

  • Party went to Quarrytown. Went to the Pavilion to pay the "tax"
  • Goazaar is allowing them to stay at his place at the moment.
  • The party went to the Toe Hold. They saw the follow customers: Kiparus Belan, a Dwarf, an unknown male, an unknown female and Jiric Helsan.
  • Gimblebok cast Speak with Animals, convinced a mouse that Jiric had a bunch of corn in his pant pockets.
    • Mouse got his family and friends, who ran up Jiric's pant legs to get to the corn.
    • Jiric left the Toe Hold.
    • Mice were not pleased to find he did not have any corn.
    • Gimblebok placated the mice with rations.
  • The following was heard at the Toe Hold
    • Marak Donar: “A couple of fellas murdered one of Rikar's Ravens and then beat it north into the woods. Maybe they headed for that Old Mine to hide out.“
    • Unknown male: “The Old Mine is haunted by evil spirits.“
    • Dwarf: “That's not what I heard, the man-wolf lives at the Old Mine“
  • Gawdor and Flint did reconnaissance at the Den of Delights
  • Gawdor talked to Kibata and asked if she was interested in being free. She was bitter, told him she had heard it before, promises, besides what would she do, where would she go?
  • Party took a long rest.

Planting 11th, 576 CY

The Old Mine

  • Party went to the Old Mine
  • Party met bear, Gimblebok talked to it.
    • Has not seen anything larger than it.
    • It had seen something about size of Delvan come and go that screams
    • Mine does go deeper.
    • It had not smelled any animal coming from deeper in the mine but has smelled gassy odors.
    • It granted permission to look deeper in mine to seek man-wolf.
