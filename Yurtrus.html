(YER-truhs)

Yurtrus the White-Handed is the terrifying orc god of death and disease. He appears as a huge, vaguely orc giant covered with peeling, rotting green flesh; his hands, however, appear completely normal except for being chalk-white in color. Yurtrus has no mouth and does not communicate; the orcs have a way of saying, "when White-Hands speaks,"" when they mean "never."

The priests of Yurtrus (called Plague Masters) wear pale white gloves made from the skins of non-orc humanoids, humans, or demi-humans during their ceremonies. They wear thin armor (equivalent to cloth) woven of the same materials. They use maces in combat, with the weapons' heads made in the shape of white fists. When plague or disease strikes a group of orcs, the priests of Yurtrus appeal to him for an end to the illness with great sacrifices of prisoners and slaves.
