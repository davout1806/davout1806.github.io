Hammerfall

 29th January 2025 at 11:38am
Holy DayPublic

Hammerfall is the most sacred day dedicated to Fortubo, the Suel lesser god of stone, metal, and mountains. It is a solemn yet joyous occasion that marks the first forging of Golbi, Fortubo’s divine hammer, and celebrates the endurance, strength, and perseverance of all those who labor beneath the earth. Hammerfall is widely observed by dwarves, gnomes, and human smiths, miners, and stonemasons who follow Fortubo’s teachings. The festival is particularly significant in regions such as Irongate, the Flinty Hills, and Lendore Isle, where the faith of Fortubo is strongest.
