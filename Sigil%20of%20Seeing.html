Sigil of Seeing

 14th March 2026 at 3:51pm
Campaign: GreyhawkDB GeneratedDnD RulePublicSpell

3rd-level divination

Casting Time10 minutes (to create a new rune) or 1 action (to reconnect to a permanent rune)
RangeSelf
ComponentsV, S, M ((charcoal or sulfur paste; 250 gp of silver for a permanent rune))
Duration1 hour per level (Concentration, up to 8 hours)

You inscribe a mystical sigil onto a surface, which becomes nearly Invisible upon completion.While active, you may switch your sensory perception between your current surroundings and the area surrounding the rune.

As an action, you can see and hear from the rune's location as if you were standing in its exact spot. You can turn your view in place but cannot move beyond the rune's location. You retain all sensory enhancements (darkvision, see invisibility, comprehend languages, tongues, etc.) but cannot cast spells or interact with objects through the rune. Detection spells (Detect Magic, Detect Evil & Good, etc.) do not function through the rune. Sensory effects can still affect you (e.g., a troglodyte's stench or a banshee's wail).

If you have previously created a permanent rune (one that was etched with silver inlay), you can attempt to reconnect to it with a DC 12 Arcana check. If successful, you can scry through the rune as if it were newly placed. If you fail, the attempt is wasted, and that rune can never be contacted again. Distance is not a factor, and the rune may even be on another plane of existence.

Detection & Countermeasures. When active, the rune functions as a scrying sensor, meaning it can be detected by:

  • Detect Magic
  • Detect Scrying
  • A DC 20 Intelligence (Investigation) check

A creature with Intelligence 12 or higher within 10 feet of the rune may notice a faint, magical presence with a DC 20 Perception check.

The rune can be disarmed or removed with:

  • DC 20 Investigation + DC 20 Thieves' Tools (for non-magical removal)
  • Dispel Magic (DC 15)
  • Physically destroying the surface it is inscribed on.

Material Components: Charcoal or sulfur paste to inscribe the rune. To make the rune permanent, it must be etched in 250 gp worth of silver inlay and requires a DC 12 Smith's Tools or Mason's Tools check. Failure ruins the materials and requires a retry.
