(LOO-thihk)

Luthic governs several spheres. She is the goddess of female orcs, orc fertility (more so for females; Gruumsh is the male fertility god), caves and caverns (which she digs herself), servitude (as she serves Gruumsh), and primitive medicine; she also helps to restore orc morale. Luthic is the deity closest to Bahgtru, her son, and he will follow her commands over all others', even those of Gruumsh. Other orc gods fear her great claws, which are so strong they can tunnel through solid rock. orc clerics call her the Cave Mother and conduct her services underground. Though she represents female orcs, males worship her as well for her ferocity and her healing powers. Orcs who follow her worship sometimes rub dirt on themselves to ensure they will have many children, and priests use earth in casting curative spells (though this is purely a symbolic gesture and not a true material component of the spell). Orcs in her tribe (that of the Vile Rune) generally dwell underground and seem to commit fewer raids against other creatures, though they are especially fierce if their lair is threatened.

Luthic appears as a huge female orc who wears no armor, but has unbreakable black claws four feet long. Her hair and eyes are dull black and her skin is dark brown, lighter on the nose and ears. When in contact with the ground, she regenerates. In addition to her normal attacks, Luthic may use magical attacks to undo her opponents. Moreover, if she hears anyone abuse her name, orc or non-orc alike, she may choose to render that being susceptible to any disease, so that the next time the victim catches even the most minor of ailments, it will prove fatal.

Luthic's worship is the only one that allows male and female half-orcs to become clerics; all of the other deities permit male priests only. Luthic's priesthood uses healing skills to tend those injured in battle and teaches simple healing and herbalism to orcs. They tend to the young and females in childbirth. They are always subservient to priests of Gruumsh. Orc guarding clerics of Luthic gain a +2 bonus "to hit" from their ferocity and madness, but this also causes a -2 penalty to their armor classes at the same time.
