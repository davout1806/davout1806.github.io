Otiluke's Diamond Screen

 14th March 2026 at 3:51pm
DB GeneratedDnD RulePublicSpell

6th-level evocation

Casting Time1 action
Range150 feet
ComponentsV, S, M (5,000 gp diamond chips)
Duration25 minutes

This spell brings into being a shimmering screen some 20' square that can be shaped in any way the caster desires (bowl, trapezoid, etc.), as long as it does not exceed the original area. Once placed, the screen cannot be moved, and will remain until dispelled, the caster wills it gone, or the duration is exceeded. Any creature passing through the screen will take 30 (3d20) hit points of slashing damage from a myriad of razor-sharp fragments from the quasi-elemental plane of mineral.
