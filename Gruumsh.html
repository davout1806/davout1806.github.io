(GRUHMSH)

The orcs have many gods, but their leader is Gruumsh. His name is never spoken by non-shaman orcs, who refer to him as He-Who-Watches or He-Who-Never-Sleeps. Gruumsh's symbol is a great unblinking eye, which represents the belief that Gruumsh watches each orc and judges him fit or unfit. Gruumsh himself has but one large eye in the center of his forehead. He appears as a huge orc wearing gleaming black plate mail, holding a flaming torch in one hand and a great iron spear in the other. The orcs say that Gruumsh commands a mighty army of spirit-orc in Gehenna, and these war continuously with a similar army of spirit-jebli controlled by Maglubiyet. The orcs always defeat the jebli, but the jebli spirits always re-form to start the battle again.

Clerics of Gruumsh must tear out their own left eyes, which gives them a 52 penalty „to hit‰ on all missile weapon attacks. The proper worship of Gruumsh requires blood in large quantities, preferably blood from a human or demi-human race. (Elves are best of all.)
