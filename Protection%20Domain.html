1st Level: Bonus Proficiency

You gain proficiency with heavy armors. You also gain proficiency in the Perception (skill).

1st Level: Bonus Cantrip

You gain the Blade Ward cantrip if you don’t already know it. Additionally, the range becomes Touch for this cantrip.

2nd Level: Channel Divinity: Saving Grace

You add your Proficiency Bonus to all saving throws of your allies within 30 feet for 1 minute.

6th Level: Channel Divinity: Warding

when a creature within 30 feet of you makes an attack roll, you can use your reaction to impose upon that creature a -5 penalty to the roll, using your Channel Divinity. You make this choice after you see the roll, but before the DM says whether the attack hits or misses.

8th Level: Channel Divinity: Mantle of Protection

You can use your Channel Divinity to bestow a mantle of protection upon your allies. As an action, choose up to three creatures within 30 feet of you (you can include yourself). The affected creatures are under the effect of the mantle of protection for 1 minute. The mantle of protection has 5 hp per level of cleric you possess plus your Wisdom modifier and your proficiency bonus. Each time a creature affected by the mantle of protection takes damage, the damage is instead applied to the mantle of protection. Once the mantle of protection is reduced to 0 hit points, it ends and any excess damage is applied to the protected creature or creatures that would have taken the full damage. When this happens, as a reaction, you can transfer all the excess damage to yourself.

17th Level: Aura of Protection

You can use your action to activate an aura of protection that lasts for 1 minute or until you dismiss it using another action. When a creature within 30 feet of you is the target of an attack that you can see, you can use your reaction to force the triggering attack to have disadvantage.
