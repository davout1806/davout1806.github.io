1st Level: Bonus Proficiencies

Beginning at 1st level, you gain proficiency in the Wisdom(Insight) and Wisdom(Perception) skills.

1st Level: Night Sentinel

Also at 1st level, you have advantage on Wisdom(Perception) checks made while keeping watch, and can spend twice the normal amount of time during a long rest keeping watch.

2nd Level: Channel Divinity: Dream Eater

Starting at 2nd level, you can use your Channel Divinity to feed off of others dreams.

As an action, you present your holy symbol and steal the dreams from a creature within 30 feet of you that is sleeping. The creature makes a Wisdom saving throw against your spell DC, taking 4d6 psychic damage on a failure, or half as much on a success. You heal an amount equal to half of the damage (rounded down) + your Wisdom modifier.

6th Level: Dreamscape

Beginning at 6th level, you can create a Dreamscape for you and your allies. When you take a short rest, you can create a dome with a 10 foot radius. Only creatures you designate can enter the sphere. Any creature in the sphere can reroll a number of hit dice equal to your Wisdom modifier (minimum of 1). Furthermore, Wisdom(Perception) checks made while inside the sphere have advantage.

8th Level: Blessed Strikes

In battle, you are blessed with divine might. When a creature takes damage from one of your spells or weapon attacks, you can also deal 1d8 radiant damage to that creature

17th Level: Nightmares

At 17th level, you can force your enemies to experience their worst nightmares, even while awake. As an action, you can choose a number of creatures you can see equal to your Wisdom modifier (minimum of 1). The chosen creatures make a Wisdom saving throw against your spell save DC. On a failure, they are frightened and paralyzed for 10 minutes. On a success, they are only frightened. They can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of their turns. You can use this feature a number of times equal to your Wisdom modifier (minimum of 1) and regain all uses after a long rest.

Fluff

Clerics of the domain of Dreams follow gods who concern themselves with sleep, dreams, insight, memory, and nightmares. These gods grant their followers peaceful sleep, dreamlike visions, and clarity in times of need, while plaguing enemies with fear, false memories, and nightmares. Clerics of the Dream domain worship gods such as Hypnos or Morpheus from Greek mythology, Nótt or Niorun from Norse mythology, or Tutu or Bes from Egyptian mythology, although any god of magic, night, or knowledge may lay claim to the Dream domain.
