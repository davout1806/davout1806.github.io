1st Level: Arcane Blessing

You get a proficiency in Arcana (skill) and you can choose any one Wizard cantrip.

1st Level: Bonus Cantrip

You can choose any one Sorcerer cantrip.

2nd Level: Channel Divinity: Regain Mana

Regain a Spell Slot of any level.

6th Level: Mysticism

select three spells of your choice from any spell list. These spells count as cleric spells for you. Additionally, when you cast a spell that requires you to make an ability check as a part of casting that spell (such as counterspell and dispel magic), you add your proficiency bonus to that ability check.

8th Level: Arcane Sight

you can cast detect magic at will, without expending a spell slot.

17th Level: Spell Resistance

you have advantage on saving throws against spells.

Fluff

Deities whose portfolios include the Magic domain govern over all or specific aspects of the power, laws and arcana lore of magic. They include deities of magic, spellcasters, mystics, schools of magic, spells, magical knowledge, magical secrets, magical artifice.
